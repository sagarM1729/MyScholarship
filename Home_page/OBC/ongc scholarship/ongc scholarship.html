ONGC Scholarship

Scholarship Details

Details

The ONGC Scholarship for Engineering supports deserving Indian engineering students pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate courses. Applicants must meet academic and income criteria, with selection based on merit and financial need. Successful candidates receive financial assistance for tuition and related expenses. Applications are typically submitted online, with renewal contingent on academic performance. For more information, visit the ONGC scholarship portal.

Benefits

  • ₹4,000 per month, totaling ₹48,000 annually

Eligibility Criteria

    • Indian citizen
    • OBC or economically backward general category student
    • Enrolled in the first year of a full-time regular program in:
      • Engineering
      • MBBS
      • MBA
      • Master's in Geology/Geophysics
    • Program must be recognized by AICTE/UGC/Association of Indian Universities/MCI/State Government/Central Government
    • Minimum 60% marks or equivalent CGPA/OGPA in 12th class for Engineering and MBBS disciplines (or graduation for PG courses)
    • Not availing any other financial assistance/scholarship
    • Age limit: Maximum 30 years as of September 1st of the application year (information based on older announcement, check for updates)
