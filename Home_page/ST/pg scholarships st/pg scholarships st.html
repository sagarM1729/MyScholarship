PG Scholarships for Professional Courses for ST

Scholarship Details

Details

PG Scholarships for Professional Courses for SC/ST 2022-23 is an initiative for candidates pursuing a postgraduate course in professional subjects. The scholarship is being offered by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Government of India. The scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to the students from the deprived section of society and provide them with an opportunity to undertake postgraduate level studies in Professional subjects like Engineering & Technology, Management, Pharmacy etc. in Indian Universities/Institutions/Colleges. The selected candidates under M.E./M.Tech. course will receive INR 7,800 per month for the duration of the PG course and students pursuing other courses will receive INR 4,500 per month.

Benefits

  • The selected candidates under M.E./M.Tech. course will receive INR 7,800 per month for the duration of the PG course and students pursuing other courses will receive INR 4,500 per month

Eligibility Criteria

  • belong to the SC/ST category
  • be pursuing a professional course at the postgraduate level
  • be studying in any of the following Institutions/Colleges/Universities:
    • Universities/Institutions/Colleges included under section 2(f) & and 12(B) of UGC Act
    • Deemed to be Universities included under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956 and eligible to receive grants-in-aid from UGC
    • Universities/Institutions/Colleges funded by Central / State Government
    • Institutes of National Importance

Application Process

  1. Click on the 'Apply Now' link below
  2. Click on the 'Register' button and fill in the required registration details. (Note: If already registered, log in using Gmail/Mobile number/Email ID).
  3. Navigate to 'Applicant Corner'and click on 'New Registration' tab.
  4. Read the details carefully, tick the checkbox and click on the 'Continue' button.
  5. Enter mobile number, captcha code, and OTP, then submit.
  6. Fill in the required details and click on 'Save & Register' to complete the registration process.
  7. Now click on the 'Fresh Application' tab and log in using the application ID and password.
  8. Fill in the required details, upload the necessary documents, and submit the application.
  9. Note:- All the applicants are advised to fill out the form correctly; no changes can be made once it is submitted. The applicants are also advised to fill out only one application, as multiple applications will cancel the form.
