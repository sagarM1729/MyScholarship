Vocational Education Maintenance Allowance to ST Students, Maharashtra

Scholarship Details

Details

Vocational Education Maintenance Allowance to ST Students, Maharashtra 2023-24 is an initiative of the Tribal Development Department, Government of Maharashtra to financially help ST students of the state who are enrolled in vocational courses. The selected candidates will receive an annual maintenance allowance.

Benefits

  • Hostellers: Upto ₹7,000 per annum
  • Day scholars: Upto ₹10,000 per annum

Eligibility Criteria

  • belong to Scheduled Tribe (ST) community of Maharashtra
  • be enrolled in a vocational course at a recognised institution
  • have an annual family income of less than or equal to ₹2,50,000
  • have passed the previous year's examination

Application Process

  1. Click on the 'Apply Now' button below.
  2. Click on the 'Register' button and fill in the required registration details. (Note:- If already registered, log in using Gmail/Mobile number/Email ID).
  3. Click on the 'New Applicant Registration' button on the right-hand side of the dashboard.
  4. Fill in all required details, and verify the email and phone number.
  5. Complete the Aadhaar authentication process either through OTP or biometrics
  6. After completing the authentication process, fill in all the required details in the 'Register' tab.
  7. Now, click on the 'Applicant login' button on the right-hand side of the dashboard
  8. Log in using the username and password.
  9. Fill in the necessary details, upload the specified documents, and submit.

Documents Required

  • Caste certificate
  • Income certificate
  • Previous year's marksheet
  • Caste validity certificate issued by a competent authority (mandatory)
  • Declaration of Rector/Superintendent of the college

Application Deadline

30th April 2024
