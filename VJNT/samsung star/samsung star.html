Samsung Star Scholar Programme

Scholarship Details

Details

The Samsung Star Scholar Programme is a prestigious scholarship initiative by Samsung India aimed at recognizing and nurturing academic excellence among meritorious students. It provides financial assistance, mentorship, and internship opportunities to outstanding students pursuing engineering and technical education in India. Recipients are selected based on their academic achievements, leadership potential, and extracurricular activities. The program serves as a platform for grooming future leaders in the field of technology and innovation. Samsung's commitment to education and talent development is evident through this initiative, which aims to empower and inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.

Benefits

  • Comprehensive financial support for deserving students
  • Covers tuition fees, accommodation, books, and other educational expenses
  • Mentorship and guidance from industry professionals
  • Opportunities for internships and career development programs
  • Access to networking events and workshops

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an Indian citizen
  • Currently enrolled in a full-time undergraduate engineering course at a recognized institution
  • Demonstrated academic excellence and leadership potential
  • Financial need may be considered but is not the sole criterion for selection
  • Must meet additional criteria set by Samsung India

Application Process

  1. Visit the official Samsung India website or designated scholarship portal.
  2. Complete the online application form with accurate details and upload required documents.
  3. Provide academic transcripts, certificates, and any other supporting documents as specified.
  4. Write a personal statement or essay outlining academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and career goals.
  5. Submit the application before the specified deadline.

Documents Required

  • Academic transcripts and certificates from previous education levels
  • Proof of enrollment in a full-time undergraduate engineering program
  • Personal identification documents (such as Aadhar card, passport, or driver's license)
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Financial documents (if applicable)
  • Any other documents specified by Samsung India

Application Deadline

  • The application deadline is typically announced on the official Samsung India website.
  • Applicants should regularly check for updates and submit their applications before the specified deadline.
