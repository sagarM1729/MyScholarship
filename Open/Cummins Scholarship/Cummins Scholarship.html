Cummins Scholarship

Scholarship Details

Details

Cummins India Foundation (CIF) has initiated the 'Cummins Scholarship Program: Nurturing Brilliance' to support academically promising students from underprivileged backgrounds in pursuing professional degree and diploma courses in Engineering.
To date, this program has awarded scholarships to 1939 students. Alongside financial assistance, selected scholars are paired with senior Cummins employees who serve as mentors, offering guidance. CIF also facilitates the enhancement of soft skills through an e-learning Softskills module.

Benefits

  • Admission fees
  • Tuition fees
  • Reimbursement of stationery of up to INR 1,500 per semester or INR 3,000 per year

Eligibility Criteria

  • be below 25 years of age
  • be currently pursuing a professional degree/diploma course in Engineering at a recognized educational institute by UGC/central government/state government OR
  • have obtained a minimum of 60% marks in senior secondary or higher secondary exams and admitted to the specified courses for further studies
  • have an annual family income of less than INR 3 lakhs from all sources
  • The students elevating to 1st, 2nd & 3rd year of Engineering (degree) OR 1st, 2nd year of Diploma can also apply. However, candidates must have scored a minimum of 60% in all their previous examinations
  • The scholarship program can be availed by students currently studying in and around Ahmednagar, Indore (Pithampur & Dewas), Jamshedpur, Phaltan & Pune only.

Application Process

  1. Click on the 'Apply Now' button and read the details carefully.
  2. Navigate to the 'Apply Now' button under 'How to Apply' section and click on it
  3. Fill in the required details to 'Register'. (Note - If the applicant is already registered, kindly login using valid credentials.)
  4. After successful registration, a confirmation email will be sent to the registered email with a username and password.
  5. Click on the activation link given in the email to access the six-step application form.
  6. Fill in all the required details and submit

  • 1st/2nd year Engineering degree/diploma/marksheet

31st December 2024
