NTPC Scholarship

Scholarship Details

Details

NTPC is an Indian public sector undertaking. It was established in 1956 and is a part of the government of India. The company deals with the generation and sale of electricity to various states. NTPC Scholarship aims to empower students and help them in every possible way irrespective of their category, gender, etc. Thereby the corporation has started a scholarship scheme for Scheduled caste/ scheduled tribe/Physically challenged students. NTPC Limited, formerly known as National Thermal Power Corporation Limited, is an Indian Public Sector Undertaking, engaged in generating electricity and allied activities. One of the norms of the NTPC Scholarship scheme is that the student must have at least 75% attendance in the classes. The student shouldn’t involve in any ragging activity. The applications for NTPC Scholarship 2023 are yet to start. The students must follow up with the official website given here. https://www.ntpc.co.in/en

Benefits

  • Candidates selected for the NTPC scholarship will receive INR 1500/- per month for the entire duration of the program.

Eligibility Criteria

  • A candidate must be pursuing a 2nd year of B.E./ B.Tech program from specified institutes.
  • The age should be of the applicant must not exceed 40 years.
  • He/ she must be belonging to SC/ ST and PC category.
  • The candidate should be domicile in Rajasthan, Delhi, J&K, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Chandigarh, and Punjab.
  • An applicant must not be receiving financial support from many sources.

Application Process

  1. The application process will be offline.
  2. Candidates can get the application form from the head of the institution or its authorized representative.
  3. Application form can also be downloaded from www.ntpc.co.in
  4. Fill in all the details correctly.
  5. Also, attach the necessary documents.
  6. Send the filled form together with necessary documents to the following address:
    Deputy General Manager (HR-W) NTPC LTD., NTPC Bhawan, Core no. -7 SCOPE COMPLEX Lodhi Road New Delhi – 110

Documents Required

  • Parent’s income certificate
  • Caste certificate
  • Identity proof
  • Address proof
  • Class 10th mark sheet (attested copy)
  • Attested Application form and photo from the college principal
  • Attested copy of the first year
  • Photocopy of passbook and Aadhar card
  • Domicile Certificate

Application Deadline

30 November
